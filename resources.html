Greetings

July 1, 2026

Welcome to each of you, past customers and prospective alike. The new season brings continued investment in seed procurement, processing, and production consistency so we can keep improving availability across the species we grow.

Demand and input costs still affect pricing, and allocation remains an important part of keeping plants available for a wide range of growers. New native offerings are marked in red in the printed catalog.

Minimum order is $200.00. Minimum shipping is $47.50 this year. If the actual cost of shipping is greater than the flat charge, the difference is billed after shipping.

This catalog assumes you know which species fit your project. Please call if you need help. We filled over 1800 orders last year and are grateful for the trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find out current availability?

We do not post availability online. Please call 269 857 7804 for an updated list.

What forms of payment are accepted?

Checks and money orders only; no credit or debit cards please.

How do you ship bare-root plants?

Plants are packed with sphagnum moss and a plastic liner in a corrugated cardboard box. Most orders ship by UPS, and large orders can ship on pallets by LTL.

How do you price plants?

Plants less than 24 inches tall are listed at the 1000 rate for a minimum of 500 per species. Plants 24 inches and taller are listed at the 100 rate.

When is the best time to order?

The best selection is generally available soon after the new catalog is released in July. Availability becomes more limited as spring shipping approaches.

Do you have an order form?

Yes. Download the printable order form.

Terms & Conditions

  • Order request quantities are adjusted to the closest standard bundle quantity.
  • 25% deposit is due with order.
  • Minimum order is $200.00.
  • Minimum shipping charge for UPS orders is $47.50.
  • Orders of 500 per species, per size, earn the 1000 rate.
  • Balance of payment is due 14 days before planned shipment unless other arrangements have been made.
  • Orders cancelled after March 15 may be assessed a 50% fee based on the changed amount.
  • All orders are subject to crop conditions including weather, inventory error, disease, and insect damage.

Alpha Nurseries sells plant material true to form and general species characteristics. Plants are guaranteed to leave the nursery in good condition, but survival after planting depends on site conditions and customer care.
