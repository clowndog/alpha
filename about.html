About Alpha Nurseries

Alpha Nurseries, Inc. is a wholesale seedling nursery located in Holland, Michigan. We grow bare-root conifers, broadleaves, trees, and shrubs for customers who need hardy woody plants in quantity.

We supply native plants for restoration, habitat improvement, development, and mitigation projects, along with non-native plants for the Christmas tree and ornamental wholesale landscape industries.

We also grow material for resale to Soil and Water Districts across the Upper Midwest and East Coast, other nonprofit organizations, and commercial plant distributors.

Alpha Nurseries grows several million plants each year across a selection of 250 species. Shipping season begins in late September and continues through the following June. Private parties are welcome to order if they can meet the minimum order.
